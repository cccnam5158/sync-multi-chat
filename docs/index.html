Powerful Features for Power Users

Everything you need to orchestrate the world's most capable AI models.

Flexible Multi-Pane UI

Interact with up to 4 services at once. Choose from 2x2, 1x3, 1x4, or specialized vertical layouts that sync with your workflow.

Simultaneous Prompting

Type once, send everywhere. A central input bar allows you to broadcast messages to all active services instantly.

AI Cross-Check

Let the AIs review each other. One click to have each service analyze and compare the responses provided by the others.

Session Persistence

Your environment is saved automatically. Restore layouts, active services, and specific URLs exactly where you left off.

Privacy First

Direct login to official services. No middleman, no credential storage. Your sessions run in isolated sandboxed environments.

Universal File Support

Drag & drop or paste images and text. Upload to multiple AIs simultaneously with a verified two-step workflow.
